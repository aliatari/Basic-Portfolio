About Me

Hello! I'm Ali, I'm a web developer with a background in product management and digital marketing.
Born and raised in Seattle, Washington, I spend as much time giving back to my community as possible.
You can expect to find me working at one of our various film festivals around town, or you might bump
into me lending a helping hand at one of the countless charity runs around the Northwest region.
When I'm not working I'm out slaying concrete, running has always been and will continue to be a major interest of mine.
I've finished 6 full marathons, 13 halfs, and too many other races to count. Not stopping till I qualify for Boston,
and even after that I'll keep going strong!
